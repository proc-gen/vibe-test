Grammar Instructions

Define your L-System using the fields below:

  • F: Move forward and draw a segment.
  • + / -: Yaw (Z-axis rotation).
  • & / ^: Pitch (X-axis rotation).
  • / / \: Roll (Y-axis rotation).
  • [ / ]: Stack operations for branching structures.

Example Rule: F -> FF+[+F-F-F]-[-F+F+F]
